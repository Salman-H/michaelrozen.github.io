  • "Hungry Dog Cafe"

Hungry Dog Cafe - menu boards, cross beam skyline piece, and hanging original works, August 2015.

This project was an opportunity to work with a local family run cafe in Vancouver, in which I was able to hang some of my work, as well as provide them with vibrant menu boards and a cross beam skyline. The Hungry Dog Cafe is located at 29th and Dunbar, and serves excellent fresh food and drinks. The works hanging in the space are available for sale, if interested please contact me through email.
